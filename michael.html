Questions Michael likely asked.

A · Your story & fit
Walk me through your background. What brought you to CS, and why Mighty Pro?

Used the 90-second intro above. Tied into Michael's four criteria.

What about Mighty Networks attracted you?

Three points: Community Design™ as a real framework; CSM as consultative, not check-in; the Host roster (Tony Robbins Arena, Mark Hyman).

Why leave ClearCareer?

Forward-looking, not complaining. Applying what you learned at greater leverage.

Tell me about a Host or customer you turned around.

Dutchie disgruntled-client story. Situation, diagnosis, the move, outcome, lesson.

B · Role mechanics
How do you approach renewal at 6 / 3 / 1 months?

6 mo: value audit. 3 mo: alignment with stakeholder. 1 mo: no surprises — execution, not negotiation.

How do you build a success plan for a new Host?

Start with their goals, not your template. Build with them, not for them.

How do you spot at-risk before it's obvious?

Champion goes quiet, engagement drops, Host stops investing on their side, conversations shift from “grow this” to “justify this.”

How do you handle a difficult-personality, high-value Host?

Separate style from substance. Not personal. Calm professional in the room.

C · Product & strategy
Your take on Mighty after spending time on it?

Loved Community Design + People Magic. Friction on tagline help and $319/mo Growth plan. Curious about Tony Robbins Skool vs. Mighty.

Ideal Mighty Pro Host vs. wrong fit?

Ideal: existing audience, clear purpose, capacity to lead. Wrong: outsourcing the work or treating community as marketing.

Differentiate Mighty Pro vs. Circle / Kajabi / Skool / custom?

Branded native apps in weeks, Community Design baked in, CSM partnership.

How do you use AI in CS day-to-day?

Otter → Claude workflow. AI buys time on high-judgement work. See AI Transparency section.

D · Self-awareness
Area you're actively working on?

Pushing back with execs more directly. Sharper commercial instincts. Going deeper vs. broad.

What manager gets the best out of you?

Context + trust. Direct feedback. Available when stuck.

Where in 2–3 years?

The CSM others come to for hard accounts. On a path toward shaping CS at portfolio level.
